Getting Started with the Parapluie Spell
In this tutorial, you'll learn about the Parapluie Spell, a handy spell for when you're out in the rain.

Getting Started
Parapluie is the incantation of a spell that creates an umbrella at the tip of the wand of its caster. It is a very simple and straightforward spell that every sorcerer learns.

Incantation: Parapluie
Pronunciation: pa-HA-plwui
Type: Spell
Light: Depends on the caster's favorite color
Description: Creates an umbrella at the tip of the caster's wand
Hand movement: Point want towards the skies
