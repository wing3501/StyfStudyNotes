Getting Started with the Water Spell

"Aquarius"

In this tutorial, you'll learn about the Water Spell, a spell that casts water out of the wand. It's a very handy spell that may even save lives.

Getting Started

The Water Spell (Aquarius), also known as the Waterfall Spell, is a spell used to generate water. The spell can be used to save lives in life threatening situations. The water generated can be drunk and can be used to extinguish fire. The caster may control the direction and quantity of water being produced.

Incantation: Aquarius
Pronunciation: uh-kweh-ree-uhs
Type: Spell
Light:Blue
Description: Creates water
Etymology: From latin means "water bearer"
Hand movement: Point wand at target
